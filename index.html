SIIM Import Experience

Sarah and Clémence - Commercial Management Assistants at SIIM, part of the Omer Decugis Group, specializing in the importation of exotic fruits. In alternation and as a duo, they explain what their job entails: importing tropical fruits from international suppliers and selling these products in France and Europe. They discuss the constraints of transportation methods (by air or sea) and their race against time when dealing with perishable products.
